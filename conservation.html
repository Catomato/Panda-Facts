Did You Know?

In China, measures are now under way to save the giant panda. To help increase its numbers, special sanctuaries have been established with sufficient space for 500-600 pandas. Both Chinese and American scientists are studying the animal’s habits and instituting conservation programmes.

Conservation: Who can help?

There are many organizations working to save these majestic creatures from extinction. Below you will find a list of conservation teams and link to their websites. Click on them to learn more about their organization and more information on pandas.

  • WWF - World Wide Fund
  • National Zoological Park Conservation
  • Chengdu Research Base of Giant Panda Breeding

Please consider donating to one of the causes above. The population of the Giant Panda has grown thanks to conservation efforts, however the panda is sadly still does not have a significant enough population.
